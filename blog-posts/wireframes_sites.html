Rdio

Rdio is a jukebox for the 21st centruy - an online music streaming service with a massive dateabase of songs that users can stream online or download to listen later, offline. There is also built in functionality for sharing and following music through social networking.

BuzzFeed

BuzzFeed is a tabloid social newsite that appears to be striving toward somewhat serious journalism and live reporting of current events while trying to maintain the entertainment content that it was founded upon.

Facebook

Facebook is THE social networking site, with 1.15 billion active monthly users, the nearest competitor is Google+, at a mere 359 million. Facebook has become more and more intergrated into our daily lives, almost every sign-up and subscription asks if you want to sign-in using your Facebook account.
