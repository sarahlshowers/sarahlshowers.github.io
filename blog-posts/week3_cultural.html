Growth Mindset Vs. Fixed Mindset

Ability is whether or not you look at something inherent that needs to be domonstrated or as something that can be developed.

Fixed Mindset

Below is a list of some of the traits of a person with a fixed mindset:

  • Belive intelligence is static
  • Have a desire to look smart
  • Give up easily
  • See effort as fruitless

Growth Mindset

Next, we will take a look at some of the trait of the growth mindset

  • Belive intelligence can be developed
  • Enbrace challenges

  • Learn from critism
